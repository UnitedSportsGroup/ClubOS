Landing Page Performance
  - Page views (total and unique)
  - Sessions
  - New vs returning visitors
  - Average time on page
  - Scroll depth (how far down the page people read)
  - Bounce rate (left without clicking anything)
  - Exit rate
  - Device breakdown (mobile / desktop / tablet)
  - Traffic source (Meta Ads / organic / direct / referral)
  - Click-through rate on the register button (CTA clicks)
  - Heatmap data (where people click and drop off)
